Welcome to Acheron

Acheron, also known as LV-426, is a moon orbiting the planet Calpamos. It is the sister moon of LV-223. Located in the Zeta II Reticuli System, it is 39 lightyears from Earth. Although Acheron has remained abandoned since the 22nd century, it has a legacy Read more about the history of Acheron here that is worth remembering. We hold hope that one day, future generations will be able to live here in peace. Acheron's hope endures!
